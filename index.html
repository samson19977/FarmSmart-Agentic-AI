💧 Water Management Guide

🌧️
Rainwater Harvesting Install catchment systems on hillsides. 1ha roof collects ~800,000L/yr at 800mm rainfall.
🚿
Drip Irrigation Saves 40–60% water vs flood irrigation. Ideal for vegetables and Irish potatoes in dry season.
🌿
Mulching Apply 5–10cm organic mulch to reduce soil evaporation by up to 50% during dry spells.
⛰️
Terracing Rwanda's hillside terraces (imidugudu) reduce runoff by 70% and prevent erosion.

🌱 Soil Improvement Practices

  • ①Compost application – 5–10 t/ha of organic compost increases soil organic matter and microbial activity.
  • ②Lime amendment – Apply dolomitic limestone (1–3 t/ha) to raise pH on acidic soils below 5.5.
  • ③Green manure – Mucuna pruriens, Tithonia, or Calliandra as cover crops between seasons.
  • ④Crop rotation – Alternate cereals with legumes to restore nitrogen naturally.
  • ⑤Minimum tillage – Reduce tillage to preserve soil structure and beneficial organisms.
